Hygiene Tips And Info



1. Change pad every 4-6 hours on a daily basis.   2.Ensure you keep supply handy at all the times.   3. Read all the instructions as a guide.


1. Wrap used pads securely in toilet paper or the wrapper provided and dispose of them in a designated bin.
2. Wash Hands - Always wash your hands before and after changing your pad to minimize the risk of infection.
3. Stay Dry - Ensure your genital area is clean and dry before applying a new pad to prevent irritation and discomfort.
4. Consider Overnight Pads - For overnight protection, consider using thicker, more absorbent pads designed for extended wear.
5. Wear Comfortable Underwear - Opt for cotton underwear, which is breathable and helps keep you dry during menstruation.
6. Be Prepared - Carry extra pads with you when you're out and about to avoid any emergencies.
