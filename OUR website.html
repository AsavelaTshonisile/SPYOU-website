About SPYOU

Our Story

SPYOU was founded in the year 2024 by a group of students at Sol Plaatje University to assist women at the university
stay comfortable and stress free during menstruation and avoid the need to go to the shops every month just to buy sanitary products.
SPYOU brings all the products at your doorsteps after you have ordered with no fee charged. We offer the following products: Pads(daytime and nightime),
tampons and feminine wipes. Visit our products page to shop our latest packages. Our goal is to sell products that provide comfort, and inspiration.
We believe that women deserve to explore the world even during their menstrual cycle and we at SPYOU help in that journey of exploration.
